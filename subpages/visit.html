Come out!

Our farm is easy to find - located right on Highway 301, three miles South of Downtown Glennville, Georgia.   This beautiful 175-acre Historic Farm is where we have 20 planted acres of Fuyu Persimmon Fruit Trees and 7 acres of Murray Cypress Christmas Trees.   We encourage our visitors to get out and enjoy the farm.  There is a marked walking trail, a beautiful 6-acre lake, picnic tables, and historic farm buildings.   We also have a gift shop where we sell products made from the persimmon fruit.   We are open seasonal hours.  And, we are also available for group tours, farm visits, presentations about the persimmon fruit, and as a farm venue.
